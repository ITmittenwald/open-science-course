This talk will discuss several tools for reproducible research and show practical examples which will make your life and lives of your colleagues easier.
A terrain modeling paper will serve as an example of sharing a full description of a published method. A landscape change model will be used to demonstrate reusable research. Finally, a paper about vegetation structure will show how all pieces of the puzzle fit together.
